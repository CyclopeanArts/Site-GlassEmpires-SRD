## End of Battle

When all members of one or both sides are defeated, or if a Round has ended where one side was fleeing, the battle is over. Fleeing sides count as defeated.

### Defeated Side

- **Defeated named characters**: Make a Mortality Save for each. If alive, captured by the enemy.
- **Defeated unnamed characters**: 50% die or desert forever; the other 50% are wounded & captured. When rounding, round towards the lighter consequence.
- **Undefeated units**: Escape without consequences.

### Victorious Side

- **Defeated named characters**: Make a Mortality Save for each, with a +2 bonus.
- **Defeated unnamed characters**: 25% die or desert forever; 25% are wounded; 50% are fine. When rounding, round towards the lighter consequence.
- **Undefeated units**: Escape without consequences.

### Battle Mortality Save Table

|       Result       | Battle Mortality Save Effects                                                                                                             |
| :-------------------: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  Crit<br />*≥12*  | You are alive & intact, with only minor wounds. You fully escape capture regardless of other rules.                                       |
|  Pass<br />*8–11*  | Reduced to 0 HP. If character, roll for a Wound; if group, all assumed wounded.                                                           |
|   Seven<br />*7*   | Reduced to 0 HP. If character, roll for a Wound; if group, all assumed wounded. You are captured by the enemy, regardless of other rules. |
| Failure<br />*3–6* | You are dead.                                                                                                                             |
|  Fumble<br />*≤2*  | You died in a particularly gruesome manner.                                                                                               |
