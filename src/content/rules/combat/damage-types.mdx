Damage may have 1 or more Damage Types. The 7 Types are:

- **Acid**: Corrosive burns.
- **Electric**: Powerful electrical currents.
- **Ice**: Hypothermic exposure.
- **Fire**: Hyperthermic exposure.
- **Mind**: Psychic, mental & emotional trauma.
- **Physical**: Slashing, puncturing, & blunt-force trauma damage.
- **Poison**: Internal toxins.

If no Damage Type is specified, it is said to be Typeless Damage.

### Resistances, Weaknesses, & Immunities

Characters may be **Weak**, **Resistant**, or **Immune** to certain Damage Types.

- **Resistance** *halves* all Damage of that Type, rounded down.
- **Weakness** *doubles* all Damage of that Type.
- **Immunity** neutralizes all Damage of a given Type.

If a source of Damage has more than 1 Type (designated with a slash, i.e. "6 Ice/Fire Damage"), the *weakest* resistance status is always used.

> For example, if a character took 6 Ice/Fire Damage & had Ice Resistance while being Weak to Fire, the character would take 12 Fire Damage (instead of taking 3 Ice Damage).

#### Typeless Damage

Typeless Damage cannot be resisted.
