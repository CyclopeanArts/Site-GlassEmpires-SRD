Glass Empires has exactly seven damage types. No Slashing, Piercing, Bleed, Cold (use Ice), Radiant, Necrotic, Sonic, Force, or Psychic (use Mind). Multi-type damage uses the target's weakest resistance. Typeless damage cannot be resisted.

### Acid

Corrosive substances and magical acid. Bypasses armor partially depending on exposure.

### Electric

Lightning, electrical discharge. Particularly dangerous in water or metal armor.

### Fire

Flame, heat, burning effects. Can ignite flammable materials and characters.

### Ice

Cold, freezing, and frost effects. Slows targets on application.

### Mind

Psychic, mental, and psychic damage. Bypasses armor entirely. Resisted by mental fortitude.

### Physical

Blunt, piercing, slashing, and impact damage. The most common damage type. Resisted by Armor.

### Poison

Toxins, venoms, and diseases. Requires exposure (injury, ingestion, inhalation). Ignores armor.

### Resistance and Vulnerability

A character may be **Resistant** or **Vulnerable** to a specific damage type:
- **Resistant**: Halves damage taken from that type.
- **Vulnerable**: Doubles damage taken from that type.
- **Immune**: Takes no damage from that type.
