## Mounted Combat

Mounts are *combatants* if they're either sapient or carnivorous. Otherwise, they're noncombatants. The following adjustments apply for mounts & their riders:

- **Saddles**: Riders without saddles must Save each time the mount takes damage or fall from a distance equal to half the mount's Size.
- **Incapacitation**: If a mount or rider is [[incapacitated|Incapacitated]] or killed, the rider(s) must Save or fall from their saddle(s). A non-combatant mount will flee battle if all riders are Incapacitated or killed.
- **Movement**: Uses the mount's scores.
- **Attacks**: Non-combatant mounts can only attack with their rider during a Charge Action. Combatant mounts get their own set of Actions on their rider's Turn.

Sapient or [[panic-fear|Panicked]] mounts may ignore their riders' wishes & take erratic action at the GM's discretion.
