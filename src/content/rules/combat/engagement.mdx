## Engagement

When you enter an enemy's reach, you become **Engaged** with them. Not as in marriage; rather, if you attempt to *leave* their reach without first taking a [[engagement|Disengage Action]], they may perform a free Strike on you with any valid weapon. Characters may perform *any number* of free Strikes in response to enemies leaving their reach without first disengaging.
