- **Races**: [[human|Human]] Glassians only (with rare [[salamander|Salamander]] exceptions)
- **Sex**: Male only (occasional exceptions made for reasons of inheritance)

You have a porcelain mask. It covers half your face. It is traced with ruby & jade & sweeping gilded lines. You were given one at birth & have worn one since that day.

You have a duty. You are the miniature [[kain|Kain]] of your domain. You are a poet, a judge, & a general. You are a murderer. You were born to rule.

And just might have, if the world hadn't fallen to pieces.

If you start as part of this Class, you are part of a Governing House, descended from the Second Phoenix's children through at least one tenuous familial link. The three main houses are Eagle, Beacon, and Arcades, but you may always invent a smaller Cadet House. There are *many* children of Kain.

## Class Features

Roll once on the table per Rank obtained. If you roll a Feature you already have, you may choose a Feature from above or below it on the list that you don't have yet.

## Class Backgrounds

If this is your first Class at character creation, roll once & write a Fact that describe a true lesson of your choice you learned from the experience: "*Because I [was (a)…], I learned [lesson].*" You can play with the phrasing so long as the background & lesson are both included.

| :-: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  1  | Pampered heir whose relatives were always trying to have me killed              |
|  2  | Never fond of the harpies my father kept as servants                            |
|  3  | Known for firing windgun warning shots at annoying petitioners in court         |
|  4  | Generous host to perennially ungrateful guests                                  |
|  5  | Signed a treaty I couldn't read to end a war I couldn't win                     |
|  6  | The Lord whose own people celebrated when I was driven out by bandits & robbers |
