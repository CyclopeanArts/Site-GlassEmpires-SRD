## 2) Choose Class

Your **Class** is your literal social position in Glass. It determines what you can become and which pools of power you can draw from.

Your **Rank** in a class is your level of achievement in it. You start with 1 Rank in any Class & may gain Ranks by increasing your commanded Factions' Level [[combat/skirmish-actions|Actions]] via *new followers* & *new conquests*.

1. **Choose one Class** from the list below to gain a Rank in, or roll randomly. Your choice determines which Races you can play, which Feature pool you draw from when gaining power, & your starting social position.
2. **Determine your Class Feature** according to that Class's means of doing so. Write it down.
3. **Roll for an Origin** on your Class's table of Origins. Write the whole thing down.
