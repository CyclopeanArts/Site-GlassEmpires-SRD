**Spellcasting** is one of the most common forms of [[what-magic-is|magic]]. It consists of prompting spirits into taking action on your behalf. In order to perform spellcasting, all you need is:

- At least one readied, non-exhausted Spell Die. You can get 1 free SD even if you have no Ranks in the Magician Class by buying some [[equipment|magician's clothes from a market]].
- A Totem containing a spell or spirit. You can source spirits by killing your enemies & [[soul-extraction|extracting their souls]]; spells can also be obtained via capture in the wild, by gaining [[magician|Magician]] ranks, or possibly by persuading other Magicians to sell you their spares.
- Enough HP to survive the consequences of the [[action-casting|Casting Action]].

The rules for spellcasting are covered by the Casting Action.
