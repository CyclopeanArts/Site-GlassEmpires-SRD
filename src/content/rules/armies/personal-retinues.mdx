You can build your own army by paying the cost for *equipment* & *training*. The cost is approximately $1,000 plus 1 MP Debt per soldier, with a training time of 1 month for green conscripts. It is performed using the [[action-make-asset|Make Asset]] Move.

Standing armies are a [[factions|Faction]] of the [[type-army|Army]] type.
