**Qualified [[classes|Classes]]**: Magician.

Your Capital is a *shrine* or *temple*, a complex of magical & spiritual artifacts & training that reflects your power & generates a [[divination|Godhead]] around your spirit. Your Families represent those committed to your temple in particular, whether half-heartedly or zealously.

You are in competition over your particular domains. Whatever your spiritual entity is committed to, anyone sharing that niche is your enemy for the sake of faction growth within a Settlement.

- **Temple**: Your Capital is a temple with a *shrine* that's dedicated to a specific entity serving as your subject of worship. So long as you exist, you support the formation & extension of a Godhead centered on that entity.
- **Expensive Maintenance**: Cults require upkeep across sacrifice, worship, maintenance of services & other factors that causes them to punch below their weight on everything that isn't related to religious or magical expertise. Take a -1 penalty to all Saves not *explicitly* relating to your Leader's Facts.
- **No Army**: You cannot take out an MP Debt against your Families — they're your customers, not your citizens. You can still hire mercenaries & guards, but you'll have to pay them directly.
