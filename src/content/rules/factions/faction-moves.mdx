A Leader can orchestrate one of their Factions into performing a Move with them, such as Resting [[skirmish-actions|Actions]] an Army or stockpiling assets [[skirmish-actions|Actions]] using the Faction's collective labor force. Many Moves are in fact designed *exclusively* for use by Leaders who are orchestrating a Faction & *require* that a Faction be involved as Factions cannot perform a Move without a Leader commanding them to do so.

When a Move is set for a Faction by its Leader, the GM writes down a **Description** of what the Move is set to accomplish adjacent to the Faction name on his list along with the **Deadline** on the calendar [[glossary-calendar|calendar]], the date on which it will be completed.
