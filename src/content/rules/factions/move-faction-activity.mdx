---

Lots of weird Faction actions won't cleanly fall into any other niche. In these cases, a GM may determine a **Deadline** based on the nature of the act & use the Success Save template table below to determine the general outcome of the Move.

At the Deadline, Save.

|      Result      | Success Save Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| :--------------: | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  Crit<br />*≥12*   | The Move succeeds twice as well as anyone hoped for, or in an exceptional way. If the outcome *directly* benefited or aided other Factions, they grow Pleased.                                                               |
|  Pass<br />*8–11*  | The Move succeeds exactly as intended.                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|   Seven<br />*7*   | The Move succeeds half as well as intended.                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Failure<br />*3–6* | A new, secondary problem has emerged that prevents the completion of the Move until resolved. Pick a Faction (*preferably an Idle one that would be opposed to the Move*) that's to blame for or related to the new problem. |
|  Fumble<br />*≤2*  | The Move fails catastrophically. The worst possible thing that could happen (for you & the local environment) happens.                                                                                                       |
