Your Capital is a *mobile encampment*. This covers nomads, merchant caravans, rogue mercenary companies, armies, and other itinerant Factions. What gets created when you raise an Army.

If you're a band of PCs in a Party & don't have a place of residence, your Faction Type is *probably* that of an army.

- **Army**: Your entire Faction is an Army composed of Soldiers, sans baggage train (which is another 25% or so, treated as invisible the same way Settlements don't count rural population).
- **Supply Lines**: If you have no explicit stockpile of food, you require an unobstructed *road* leading back to a Settlement with as many Families as you have soldiers at all times. If the supply line is ever disrupted (i.e. by enemy forces blocking all valid paths), you must *immediately* roll an Exhaustion Save & make a new one each week.

Armies may or may not have a Families stat representing noncombatants.
