A **silver piece** ($1) represents one day of unskilled labor & is the smallest denomination of coin in circulation. Most things are paid for with debt (p. @), typically via the gift economy. An asset's price in \$ is said to be its **Value**.

## The Gift Economy

You are gifted what you need *today*, expecting that *tomorrow* you will return the favor. Not doing so is *dishonorable*. Coincidentally, travelers & transients are dishonorable because you cannot ever hold them to a promise the way you & your village can hold your neighbor to account. If you need to track a debt explicitly, use the Debt system (p. @).

## Converting from Other Systems

The best way to convert prices from other game systems' price lists is to find the daily labor-value & convert based on that ratio. Be warned that many game designers have little grasp on finance or economics & that their assumptions may not hold steady across systems or settings.
