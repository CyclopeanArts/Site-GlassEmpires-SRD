## Taxonomy

- **Actor**: An entity capable of taking action.
  - **Character**: A featherless biped (or otherwise independent entity).
    - **PC**: A character controlled by a Player.
    - **NPC**: A character controlled by the GM.
    - **Leader**: A character who controls & represents a Faction.
    - **Retainer**: A character who is suborned to another for some reason (hired, sworn servants, friends, slaves).
    - **Liege**: A Retainer's master.
  - **Faction**: A statistical grouping of many characters taking collective action.
    - **Subfaction**: A Faction whose Leader is the Retainer of another Leader.
