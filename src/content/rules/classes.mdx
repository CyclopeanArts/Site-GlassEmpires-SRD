Your **Class** is your literal social position in [[island-of-glass|Glass]]. It determines what you can become and which pools of power you can draw from.

Your **Rank** in a Class is your level of achievement in it. At the start of the game, your new PC starts with 1 Rank in any Class & may gain Ranks by increasing your commanded [[what-is-a-faction|Factions']] Level via *new followers* & *new conquests*.

1. **Choose one Class** from the list below to gain a Rank in, or roll randomly. Your choice determines which Races you can play, which Feature pool you draw from when gaining power, & your starting social position.
2. **Determine your Class Feature** according to that Class's means of doing so. Write it down.
3. **Roll for an Origin** on your Class's table of Origins. Write the whole thing down.

| 1d6 | Class      | Description                                                  |
| :-: | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
|  1  | [[lord\|Lord]]       | Porcelain-masked ruler. Born to command, raised to kill.     |
|  2  | [[magistrate\|Magistrate]] | The machinery of Glass. Law, tax, and records made flesh.    |
|  3  | [[magician\|Magician]]   | Spirit-speaker and god-keeper. Power bought with blood.      |
|  4  | [[burgher\|Burgher]]    | Merchant-prince. He who commands commerce commands kings.    |
|  5  | [[warlord\|Warlord]]    | King-slayer and contractor. The disruption made profession.  |
|  6  | [[gangster\|Gangster]]   | Smuggler, racketeer, underworld financier. Solves problems.  |
