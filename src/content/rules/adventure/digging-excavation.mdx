title: "Digging & Excavation"
tags: [rules, adventure]
draft: false
summary: "Moving earth, breaking stone, and construction rates."
---

Characters can move cubic feet of earth equal to their Max. Load in # per hour, assuming appropriate tools are possessed. The rate is halved for improvised tools, and quartered for a lack of them. Dressed stone brick & smooth rock takes twice as long to excavate.

Construction projects are performed with the Make Asset Move [[factions/move-make-asset|Make Asset Move]]. Excavation costs are based on material; see the associated Move for details.
