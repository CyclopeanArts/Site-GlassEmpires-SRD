**Short Rest**: A brief pause of up to an hour. Restores no HP but allows characters to catch their breath, bind minor wounds, eat, and prepare spells or Totems.

**Long Rest**: A full night's sleep (6-8 hours) in a safe, comfortable location. Restores all HP, recovers all Spell Dice, and clears most Conditions.

**Unsafe Rest**: If sleeping in armor, on rough ground, or in a dangerous area, characters regain half HP and may not recover all Conditions at the GM's discretion.

The [[Resting|Rest Move]] (p. @) provides the mechanical procedure for structured rest within the faction turn system.
