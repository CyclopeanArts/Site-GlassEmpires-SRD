Conditions are temporary [[facts|Facts]] that apply a set of specialized rules to a character or scene for a period of time. You can see the full list of conditions at the [[conditions|Conditions Index]].

Common conditions referenced in the adventure rules include:

- [[condition-encumbered|Encumbered]] — halved travel speed, -1 on dexterity/agility Saves
- [[condition-panicked|Panicked]] — fleeing in terror, doubled damage taken
- [[condition-prone|Prone]] — on the ground, certain action restrictions
- [[condition-grappled|Grappled]] — locked in close combat

More detailed condition rules are available on each condition's page.
