See the full list of conditions at the [[conditions|Conditions Index]].

Common conditions referenced in the adventure rules include:

- [[encumbered|Encumbered]] — halved travel speed, -1 on dexterity/agility Saves
- [[panicked|Panicked]] — fleeing in terror, doubled damage taken
- [[prone|Prone]] — on the ground, certain action restrictions
- [[grappled|Grappled]] — locked in close combat

More detailed condition rules are available on each condition's page.
