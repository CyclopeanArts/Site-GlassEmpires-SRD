Make a non-commodity physical asset, such as a statue, building, fortress, bridge, or road.

The **Deadline** is equal to the asset value in $ times 6, divided by your number of Families.

Alternatively, you can invest $5 per month (or ~$0.17 per day) per Family to calculate the Deadline.

The *minimum* Deadline is 1 day per $1,000. Even if you have liquid capital (i.e. silver, gold) on hand, expensive assets require extensive negotiations as well as search & construction periods.

At the Deadline, Save.

|      Result      | Success Save Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| :--------------: | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  Crit<br />*≥12*   | The asset is complete on time & clever management has eliminated the debts involved in the asset's creation (if any were made through prior cost overruns).                                                                                                                                  |
|  Pass<br />*8–11*  | The asset is complete on time.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|   Seven<br />*7*   | There were cost overruns. You can either find a valid Faction who will loan you **10%** of the asset's value within the next week to complete it at the time of the loan, or set a new Deadline to finish the project using the remaining cost to pay as the basis for determining Deadline. |
| Failure<br />*3–6* | There were cost overruns. You can either find a valid Faction who will loan you **50%** of the asset's value within the next week to complete it at the time of the loan, or set a new Deadline to finish the project using the remaining cost to pay as the basis for determining Deadline. |
|  Fumble<br />*≤2*  | The construction fails catastrophically. You lose 10% of your Followers; roll for a Random Faction Event & interpret it as the worst possible consequence of the failed efforts.                                                                                                             |

<Callout type="gm" heading="How do I make an asset when I don't rule a Faction?">
Characters may treat their job as a Fact. Similarly, applicable Facts (i.e. greed, thriftiness) may indicate higher production capacity than your Faction's Followers otherwise suggests. In addition, if you have a liquid sum (such as silver dollars you plundered), you can simply dump that into an Asset.

Then again, even a master blacksmith is Level 3 — he has journeymen & apprentices under him — and a fundamental tenet of Glass Empires is that *no man is an island*. If you need better production capacity, the best place to get it is to learn to command.
</Callout>
