# Religion & Gods


### An Ecosystem of Gods

Aia is home to "an ecosystem of gods." Everyone maintains a basic relationship to the gods that matter most to them — rice, water, war, threshold, ancestral spirits. There's no need to give a +1 for it.

A god is an actual creature with creature-like powers, constrained by their physical nature. They are often not worshiped outside of their center of activity. Demigods are generated as monsters that occupy Locations on the map — roughly 1 per 5-10 settlements. Many demigods are humans that have acquired a lot of features over time.

### Kain's Cult

The commoners worship Kain — who now resides in the **Oropolis**, the temple-mountain at the heart of Glass near **Theomni**. His cult is the established faith. But many now say it has failed. The gods are killing everyone.

### The Five Gods of Glass

The **Five Gods of Glass** are the only figures other than Kain himself who could force the island to its knees before a new crown. They are agents of renewal & rebirth. Rejuvenation is their prime symbol. They approved the crowning of the Saint Emperor.

### The Twintail Cult

There is a twintail-worshiping cult that was originally encouraged & fostered by Kain. Yes, the hairstyle. They have a dedicated art to the twintail spirits they foster & manage. They insist on the existence of a deity they call **Solar**, who may or may not be real.

> Note: While faith *can* warp reality on Aia, it takes a certain critical mass to create a god ... or an originating mortal soul.

### Reincarnation

Glass **renews itself**. Kain's heraldry is the **PHOENIX**. Glassians sing in rhyme & dance to their festivals well into the night because:

> WE  
> WILL  
> REINCARNATE

Up until now, every hymn and verse has been cyclical. Ages pass, and all that is fades to dust, but patterns remain, and Glass is the grandest of patterns. And now the tremor in society is that *we won't reincarnate.* That is the fear, and no small number of the old rites have been neglected in the chaos.
