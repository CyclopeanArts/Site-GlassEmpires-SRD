# Economy & Debt

Glass sits at a strange intersection: it was "intended" that the world be a Renaissance or somewhat post-Renaissance setting, with increasing clockwork/elemental steampunk-ish components. The industrial revolution is approximately one steam engine away.

### The Green Revolution

Magic is increasingly frequent. There are entire university cities dedicated to the arts of spirit-wrangling — this is Papermark's claim to fame. A Green Revolution is under way, as agricultural magic becomes affordable & widely deployed.

### The Debt Crisis

Capitalistic theory is spreading. The Venetians developed the theory & imported it to Glass around 30 years ago — shortly before the Coralers struck with windguns & an almost comical viciousness. Joint-stock corporations were possible, but the empire started going through a normal imperial cycle of decay starting with the Abdication.

The result is a severe terminal empire crisis. Debt, no more tributaries. The island is flooded with foreign mercenaries & operations from the two other notable foreign powers — Hydrea and Venice. The ranks of the indentured servants are swelling with formerly free men. Everyone's a slave, broken, fleeing, or joining General Ram.
