- **Leader**: Mother Empress
  - A possessed & possibly dead madwoman regent possessing terrible magical power, arrayed by a host of demons.
- **Capital**: City of Leaves (15,200)

## Assets

- **Sūn, Prince of Glass**: The 6-year-old heir to the throne. Recognized as the legitimate heir by everyone who doesn't have a stake in potentially claiming the throne for themselves.
- **The Curtain**: The City of Leaves is built inside two massive concentric granite walls of nearly impervious engineering. The outer wall once held a substantial amount of farmland, but urban sprawl over time has resulted in its internal production being too low to support the population indefinitely. The Curtain is the only thing keeping the Regency in power.
