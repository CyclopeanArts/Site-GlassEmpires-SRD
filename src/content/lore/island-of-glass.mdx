# The Island of Glass


<img src="/assets/island-of-glass-beta.webp" alt="Map of the Island of Glass" style="width: 100%; height: auto; max-width: 600px; display: block; margin: 0 auto 1.5em auto; border-radius: 4px;" />

Glass is an island roughly half the size of Honshu, triangular in shape, with a long mountain range on its counterclockwise side and a smaller cluster of peaks to its clockwise point. The land between forms a raised valley — temperate, fertile, and increasingly scarred by war.

This section covers everything about the island itself: its geography and climate, its distinctive architecture (from lowland timber to highland stone to subterranean salamander warrens), the four strategic wonders that shape the conflict, and the as-yet-uncatalogued biomes that players will travel through.
