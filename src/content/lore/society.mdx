# Society & Culture


Glassians believe they are unique among all people on Aia: they are immortal, reborn after death like their god-emperor Kain. They are also porcelain-white, slender & tall, and deeply proud of their island's beauty — the rivers run with ruby-crusted sands, the birchwood sprawl paints the land, and nobody has ever made finer statuary, pottery, or timberwood structures.

But the war is eating everything. The festivals are dying out. The ranks of the indentured servants are swelling with formerly free men. And a creeping doubt haunts every feast and hymn: *Are we really immortals?*
