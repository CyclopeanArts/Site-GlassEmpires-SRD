## Custom Magical Schools

Magicians mechanically consist of a **Magical School** possessing a mixture of **Class Features** & a **Spell Tradition**. The means & methods by which these are obtained varies.

In narrative terms, magicians have a **philosophy** that encompasses the way they see & interact with the world. The art of dealing with spirits is intrinsically tied to your mindset as a practitioner of magic.

### Class Features

Magician Class Features tend to be double-edged swords relating to their nature in reality.

Chosen via method:

- Linear
- Random
- Choice of selection

### Magical Traditions

Each magical school has a *tradition* of 12 spells based on the most fundamental aspects of how they interact with & view the world.

Traditions represent *minor* spirits; grease elementals, the spirit of an echo, a newborn god with nothing to offer. These spirits are common & easy to locate, or else they *deliberately seek out* magicians of the appropriate mentality & presentation.

Most magicians can capture & sell these traditional spells fairly easily. It takes about a month of effort. It is not a tremendously profitable market.

More complex, powerful & dangerous spells come from the beast markets or from hunting.
