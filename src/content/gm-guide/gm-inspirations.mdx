While I include some works of fiction & nonfiction alike here for reference in comparison to the game, I must note that the vast majority of the inspiration has derived from my personal life & from research into matters far beyond the scope of any particular work. In a real sense, the philosophy that undercuts the design of Glass Empires is a reflection of my own psyche, and the system is a way of mapping the real world to my personal satisfaction. There are very few works of art, I find, that deal with the blunt knife's edge that is power & its expressions. For this, the *Dune* series is unparalleled.
