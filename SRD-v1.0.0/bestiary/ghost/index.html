Ghost

spiritundeadtrapephemeralhazard

StatsHD 4 (16)BR N/ASize 0'Weight 0#Max. Load 0#
Group1 solo
Desc.If as anything at all, a looping psychic recording of an unfortunate moment. Flickering, broken apparitions of distended body parts & warped faces. May oscillate between dead silence, quiet whispers, & chilling shrieks.
WantsYou to meet its fate.
IntellectEssentially absent.
MoralityNo.
  • Illusion: You are not incorporeal. You aren't even undead. You don't exist at all. You are a projection of light & sound from a corpse-body serving as a Totem, housing a trapped & tortured spirit. If your Totem is destroyed, you revert to being a normal loose spirit.

Ghosts aren't monsters. They're psychic splatters of horrible violent deaths ... or deliberate traps made by unscrupulous magicians. Their manifestation is a shattered vision of whatever transpired.

A ghost is composed of 4 components: The Totem, generally within 30'; the premonition, an emotional or physical flash of warning; the effect, the onset symptoms; and the satiation, a means of ending the effect.

  • Destruction: Destroying the Totem-corpse will end an effect, but place a curse upon the defiler.
  • Holding a Funeral: A Psychopomp Totem will be attracted to the funerary rites; they will handle the ghost.
  • Clever Means: If a Party comes up with an innovative solution to a curse that thematically resolves the ghost's fate, it may be treated as satiation. In this sense, the listed satiations may be considered suggestions rather than iron requirements.

Treasure: Sometimes, a dead body to be looted .

Ghost Manifestations
d20Ghost Manifestations
1–2Death: Abandoned.
Premonition: Lights leaving, laughter fading. Crushing loneliness creeping in.
Effect: Lights slowly go out; Party quickly grows deaf.
Satiation: Bring ghost's corpse to the surface, or to friends' corpses, or leave another nearby.
3Death: Beheaded.
Premonition: A sharp, tearing pressure on one side of your neck that builds rapidly. Visions of guillotines & scythes.
Effect: Blood starts leaking, a cut forms rapidly. Fatal in 2d6+2 Rounds without extreme magic.
Satiation: Reattach head of ghost's corpse to its body.
4Death: Betrayed.
Premonition: Hear whispers from party, paranoia creeping. They're plotting against you.
Effect: Flee from the Party in whatever direction the Party is least familiar with. Cannot stop until you're at least 10 minutes away.
Satiation: Remove the weapon from ghost's corpse, or destroy betrayer's corpse (if present).
5Death: Betrayer.
Premonition: Envious, hateful gaze upon least liked ally.
Effect: Sneak attack them at nearest opportunity.
Satiation: Destroy the ghost's corpse.
6Death: Buried Alive.
Premonition: Pressing, straining against stone coffin roof.
Effect: Sink slowly underground over 1d6+1 Rounds.
Satiation: Remove ghost's corpse from sarcophagus.
7Death: Burnt.
Premonition: Searing heat, blazing hot air and smoke.
Effect: You suffer 1d6 Fire Damage per round.
Satiation: Soak ghost's corpse in water.
8Death: Crushed.
Premonition: Walls pressing in, extreme claustrophobia. The ceiling is coming down on you.
Effect: Every round, your Speed scores are halved. At 5' or less, take 1d6 Damage per Round.
Satiation: Reassemble ghost's corpse. Takes 1d6+1 full turns split between up to 4 participants.
9Death: Drowned.
Premonition: Choking, gasping, sinking down & down. The bubbles of your last breath slipping.
Effect: Fall into the water. You are unable to leave it. It will rise to drag you back in if you try.
Satiation: Remove ghost's corpse from the water.
10Death: Falling.
Premonition: Horrible vertigo, painful aching in your limbs and bones.
Effect: All attempts to climb fail at around their half-way point. Grips slip, pitons loosen.
Satiation: Reassemble ghost's corpse, twist bent parts back into shape.
11Death: Freezing.
Premonition: Heat rushing out from your limbs, the crack of ice underneath. Your fire doesn't aid you. You're so cold.
Effect: Hands lock up, unable to hold anything. Take [HD] Ice Damage every 10 minutes.
Satiation: Move ghost's corpse near a source of heat, or set it ablaze.
12Death: Impaled.
Premonition: Sudden wet shlick and crunch of something stabbing through your ribs.
Effect: Pinned in place by a heavy, painful, penetrating pressure upon your chest.
Satiation: Remove ghost's corpse from source of impalement.
13Death: Imprisoned.
Premonition: Weighty iron digs into your ankles & wrists. You are going to die here.
Effect: Your wrists & ankles are unable to leave the room.
Satiation: Cut the iron chains binding the ghost's corpse.
14Death: Lost Item.
Premonition: Frightening vision of having lost a treasure. It's down here somewhere, it has to be...
Effect: A random item disappears from your pack (but not your hands) every 10 minutes.
Satiation: Return envisioned item to ghost's corpse. Items are found in a pile soon afterwards.
15Death: Parasite.
Premonition: Something's growing inside you. You can feel it. You know it's there. It has to die, before it eats you inside-out.
Effect: Every 10 minutes, Save or Panic & drink poison & rip at your flesh & stomach to get the parasite out.
Satiation: Find and kill a member of the species that infested the ghost's corpse.
16Death: Petrified.
Premonition: Heavy pressure on your skull; reaching out for something and never touching.
Effect: Limbs lock up and petrify as rounds pass. Only continual human touch restores you to life.
Satiation: Move the ghost's statue to whatever it was trying to reach before it was petrified.
17Death: Terrified.
Premonition: Sharp, sheer terror that cripples and shocks you. Overwhelming paralysis.
Effect: 1d6 Mind Damage per round. At 0 HP, you suffer a heart attack & die.
Satiation: Physically comfort the ghost's corpse as you might a scared child (or equivalent).
18Death: Sacrificed.
Premonition: Black shouted chanting, wisps of oil smoke, the rest of a dagger's tip over your heart.
Effect: Victim compelled to lie down on altar, heart carved and ripped out after 1d6+1 Rounds.
Satiation: Reassemble nearby jarred organs inside ghost's corpse, or sacrifice an ally/treasure.
19–20Death: Starving.
Premonition: A gnawing, ravenous hunger intensifies by the second. You haven't eaten anything in weeks.
Effect: Eat every 10 minutes. If you skip a meal, lose [HD] HP from starvation.
Satiation: Place food inside the ghost's corpse.
