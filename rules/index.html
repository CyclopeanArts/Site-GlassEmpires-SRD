Rules

  • (Move) Capital Relocation — Relocating your seat of government to a new Settlement or Location.
  • (Move) Casting — Expending life energy on captive spirits to wield their power — spell-casting and soul-casting.
  • (Move) Create Subfaction — Promoting a Retainer, gifting a Capital, and forming a new Subfaction.
  • (Move) Faction Activity — Resolution for unusual Faction actions that don't fit a predefined Move.
  • (Move) Grow Follower Base — Converting population within a settlement to your faction.
  • (Move) Make Asset — Building statues, fortresses, bridges, and roads.
  • (Move) Plunder a Settlement — Looting settlements with an army.
  • (Move) Sabotage — Espionage, debt, political conflict, and indirect warfare.
  • Advancement & Leveling Up — Gaining levels and improving your character.
  • Armor & Armor Points — How armor works — Armor Points (AP) and damage reduction.

  • Battle Procedure — Units, Round Duration, and the 6-step Battle Round procedure.
  • Battle Rating — BR formula — Power, Armor Multiplier, Damage Multiplier, and Feature multipliers.

  • Burgher — The Burgher class.
  • Business — Mercantile and artisan ventures.

  • Personal Retinues — Building custom armies — equipment costs, training times, and ongoing maintenance.

  • Resting — Recovering from exertion and wounds via the Resting Move.
  • Retainers & Hirelings — Availability by settlement, the Retainer Job Table, and monthly wages.

  • Skirmish Actions — All skirmish actions — Attack, Cast, Dash, Sprint, Charge, Interact, and more.
